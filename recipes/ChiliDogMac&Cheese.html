Chili Dog Mac & Cheese Recipe


Chili Dog Mac & Cheese Result

According to the person who originally made this recipe, this dish didn't really blend the flavors well together. It could have been the lack of creamy sauce, or something else. The texture was definitely there, but there was definitely some gaps in the recipe.

If you'd like something that feels and tastes like an American Goulash with hot dogs, then I would recommend this for your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano, preferably Mexican oregano
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup diced green peppers
  • 1 pound hot dogs, sliced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes (or 3 1/2 cups tomato sauce or puree)
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 2 rounded cups elbow macaroni
  • 8 ounces shredded white cheddar cheese
  • 8 ounces shredded monterey jack cheese
  • green onions, sliced, for the top (optional)
